About Us

Research and Development Partners (RDP) is a community-based organization committed to promoting sustainable development through research, innovation, youth empowerment, and social impact initiatives. We work closely with communities, institutions, and development partners to deliver practical solutions to local challenges.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To strengthen communities by using research-driven approaches, capacity building, and partnerships that foster inclusive growth and sustainable livelihoods.

Vision

A resilient and empowered society where evidence-based development improves quality of life for all.

Our Core Programs

Youth Empowerment

Skills training, entrepreneurship support, and job creation initiatives for young people.

Research & Policy Support

Community research, needs assessments, and policy-oriented studies to inform decision-making.

Community Development

Education support, health awareness, and livelihood projects at grassroots level.

Key Projects

Soft Heart Hub – Youth Empowerment Initiative

A social enterprise project aimed at empowering over 100 youths within two years through plastic recycling and water production, creating sustainable jobs and reducing environmental waste.

Academic Aid for Early Childhood Education

Provision of school bags, books, and learning materials to under-five school-going children across selected schools, supporting access to quality education.
